Fun Fact: Mere Exposure Effect!

Repetition breeds familiarity, and familiarity breeds liking! Keep exposing your kids to new music, and watch their tastes evolve.

The more you're exposed to something, the more you tend to like it! This principle, known as the Mere Exposure Effect, explains why your kids start to like a song they once hated after hearing it a few times. Keep playing that good music--eventually, they'll come around!
