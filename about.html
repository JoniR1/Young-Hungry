About the Podcast

Dr. Sean Young

Dr. Sean Young is a behavioral psychologist, professor, and author of the #1 Wall Street Journal bestseller "Stick With It." He is the Executive Director of the UC Institute for Prediction Technology and has held roles at NASA, Stanford, and UC Irvine, specializing in behavior change and digital health. The podcast Young & Hungry explores why talented kids excel in math, art, and entrepreneurship. Through fun facts and real-life stories, the podcast inspires children to pursue their passions and helps parents and educators foster their growth.
